# SINTER_EXTENSIVE_SPEC.md - The African Digital Architecture

## 1. THE VISUAL AURA (THE "SHIMMER" INTERFACE)
*   **Color Space:** "Deep Obsidian" (`#020202`) with variable-depth opacity.
*   **The Sinter Pulse:** The logo doesn't stay static; it "blinks" in the shadows of the UI, using a slow-breath neon glow (`#00f0ff` to `#ff00f0`).
*   **The Grid:** A faint, kinetic hexagonal or isometric grid that reacts to touch/cursor, representing the "Lattice."

## 2. PHASE-LOCK (ONBOARDING & IDENTITY)
Sinter treats identity based on "Proximity to the Core."
*   **African Continent Residents:**
    *   **Method:** Seamless Mobile Number + OTP (Fast-path).
    *   **Logic:** Mobile-first, low-friction, capitalizing on high mobile penetration.
*   **External/Diaspora/Foreign Entities:**
    *   **Method:** Mandatory ID Verification (Regulatory-path).
    *   **Logic:** Ensures high-trust inputs from outside the continent and meets international compliance.
*   **Initial Status:** All new logins start as **"Silent Nodes"** (Write-access only, limited broadcast).

## 3. THE FLUX ALGORITHM (INTENT-BASED VISIBILITY)
Traditional algorithms favor "Engagement" (anger/clickbait). The **Sinter Flux** favors "Intent."
*   **The AI Gatekeeper:** Before any node (user) achieves high-visibility broadcast, the internal AI (Sinter-Seer) analyzes their early "Pulses."
    *   **Quality/Intent Score:** Is the node contributing to the Zone? Is the work real? 
*   **Visibility Progression:**
    *   **Level 0 (New Node):** **Area-Local IP Views.** You only show up to people in your immediate physical/digital vicinity (Local Lattice).
    *   **Level 1 (Verified Intent):** **Zone Expansion.** Your pulses reach the broader Resonance Zone (e.g., the whole West African fiber-loop).
    *   **Level 2 (Lattice-Authenticated):** **Continental/Global Broadcast.** Full visibility, video-upload capability unlocked.
*   **Node Hierarchy:**
    *   **New Node:** Basic Pulse (Text/Image).
    *   **Verified Node:** Enhanced Pulse (Video/Streams/Jobs).
    *   **Government Node:** Official "Apex" status with dedicated "Registry" spaces for announcements/service-delivery.

## 4. THE SOCIAL MANIFOLD (THE FEED)
*   **Fluid Motion:** Posts don't just "scroll"; they "flow" in a non-linear arrangement, prioritizing the visual weight of the content.
*   **Space Allocation:**
    *   **The Registry:** Dedicated sidebar/overlay for Government/Regulatory nodes (Taxes, Public Health, Infrastructure updates).
    *   **The Manifold:** The main feed of pulses from your Zone.
    *   **The Task-Pool:** The integrated job-market view.

## 5. REVENUE & STATUS
*   **Activate Node Status (Premium):** 
    *   Unlocks **High-Fidelity** rendering of the UI.
    *   Grants priority in the **Task-Pool** (faster job matching).
    *   Allows **Sub-Lattice Archiving** (extended message/post history).

## 6. TECHNICAL DIFFERENTIATION
*   **IP-First Routing:** Unlike Twitter, Sinter optimizes data-routing internally. If you are in a low-bandwidth zone, Sinter serves "Pulse-Lite" (text/compressed images) automatically until a stable node is reached.
