# Sinter: The Multi-Dimensional Social & Career Layer for Africa

## 1. Core Identity: Beyond Geography
*   **Zones, Not Countries:** Traditional borders are static. Sinter uses **Active Zones** (based on economic resonance, fiber-optic latency, or cultural clusters).
    *   *Example:* Instead of "Nigeria," you are in the **Lagos-Accra Resonance Zone**.
*   **The Terminal Aesthetics:** Every UI element feels like a high-end futuristic OS (Glassmorphism, Neon/Ultraviolet, Mono-font interfaces).

## 3. The "Real Job Interface" (The Kinetic Career Layer)
Instead of a static resume (LinkedIn style), Sinter uses **Proof-of-Work Visualization**:
*   **The Work-Pool:** A live, 3D manifold where "Jobs" are "Tasks" floating in a void. You don't apply; you **Commence Processing**.
*   **Visual Reputation:** Your skills aren't bullet points. They are a **Braid** (inspired by your Flux research). The more you work, the thicker your "Skill Braid" becomes.
*   **Instant Settlement:** Payments via decentralized rails (Stablecoins/Local-resilient tokens) so African talent gets paid without the "3-7 business days" delay.

## 4. Hardware/Network Adaptations (Africa-First)
*   **Mesh-Fallback:** Sinter should have a "Low-Power Mode" that can broadcast via Bluetooth/LoRa if the main grid goes down.
*   **Voice-First Interface:** For regions with oral-rich cultures, "Pulses" can be voice-transcribed with AI in local dialects.
