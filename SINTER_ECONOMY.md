# SINTER_ECONOMY.md - The Kinetic Value Layer

## 1. THE CURRENCY: SINT (Sinter Tokens)
SINT is the native utility token of the Sinter lattice. It represents "Verified Computational or Creative Work."

*   **Earning SINT:**
    *   **Architect Pool:** Completing tasks (Manifold optimization, shader builds, registry patches).
    *   **Resonance Rewards:** If a Pulse reaches "High Resonance" (>1k verified resonators), the node earns a small SINT fraction.
    *   **Node Hosting:** Nodes that provide stable relay services for their local area earn passive SINT.
    *   **Government Grants:** Official tasks issued by "Apex Nodes" for social utility (e.g., data mapping, civic engagement).

## 2. THE DUAL-PAYMENT SYSTEM
Users can fund their "Node Status" or pay for Sinter-Market items using two tracks:

*   **Fiat Track (The "Local Lattice"):**
    *   Integration with **Mobile Money** (M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Wave) and local cards.
    *   Optimized for users who want to "Activate Node Status" without touching crypto.
*   **Coin Track (The "Global Lattice"):**
    *   **SINT Tokens:** Earned or traded.
    *   **External Assets:** BTC, ETH, and Stablecoins for high-value transactions or cross-border Architect payments.

## 3. NODE STATUS TIERS (The Economy of Visibility)
*   **Standard Node (Free):**
    *   Basic Pulse access.
    *   Limited broadcast range (Local IP).
*   **High-Fidelity Node (SINT or Fiat Monthly):**
    *   Unlocks **Video Broadcast**.
    *   **Priority Processing** in the Task-Pool (see jobs first).
    *   **Global Broadcast** range enabled (Intent-check still applies).
    *   Unique **Neon-Chrome** UI skin.

## 4. ARCHITECT COMMISSIONS
*   When an Architect completes a task for a private company or a government node, the payment is escrowed in SINT.
*   Sinter takes a **1.06% Lattice Fee** to maintain the Fluid OS infrastructure.

## 5. THE MANIFOLD MARKETPLACE
*   Digital assets (Fluid OS themes, shader kits, Braid presets) can be sold by Architects to other nodes.
*   Physical goods (localized for Africa) can be purchased using a mix of SINT and Fiat.
